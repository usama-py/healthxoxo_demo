DIET CHART

A diet-chart is a set of guidelines for what one should or shouldn't eat for a healthy life. Diet Charts can be used to reference the nutritional information of various foods and to provide recommendations, guidelines, and ways to track a diet. In order to lead a healthy life, it is essential to follow a balanced diet. A balanced diet is associated with good health, prevention of diseases, and recovery from illness.
