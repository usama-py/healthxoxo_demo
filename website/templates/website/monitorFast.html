Monitor Fasting

Fasting has been practiced for centuries for both religious and health purposes. While religious fasts are primarily intended for spiritual purposes, fasting for health benefits has gained popularity in recent years. Fasting for health benefits can take many forms, including intermittent fasting, alternate-day fasting, and extended fasting. Fasting has been linked to a range of health benefits, including weight loss, improved insulin sensitivity, and improved cardiovascular health.
